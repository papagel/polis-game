Why sim fans love it

Every system you remember from the classics — RCI demand, land value, traffic, services, budgets, disasters — running honestly under the hood, with no shortcuts and no microtransactions.

A real economy

Population, jobs, commerce and industry chase each other through supply and demand. Raise taxes too high and growth stalls. Every number is connected.

🏙️

Buildings that grow

Zone a block and lots level up 1→7 on their own — then four mature lots merge into a tower that can climb to a skyscraper, highrise, and megatower.

Traffic that bites

Cars actually drive your roads. Gridlock chokes land value and growth — so you fight back with avenues, buses, and elevated rail.

Networks to manage

Roads carry power and water through the whole grid. Plan plants, pumps and reach, or watch unpowered blocks wither and downgrade.

🗺️

See the invisible

Flip overlays for traffic, density, land value, pollution, power, water, crime, education and health — and read your city like an X-ray.

🎚️

Govern with trade-offs

Funding sliders, ordinances, loans and tax rates. Recycling, curfews, festivals, tolls — every policy is a cost weighed against a payoff.
